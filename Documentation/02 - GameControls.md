# PC - Keyboard / Mouse

### WASD
- movement forward and back
- Strafe left and right

### MOUSE
- Aim / Look
- Left click - shoot

# Controller

### LEFT JOYSTICK
- movement forward and back
- Strafe left and right

### RIGHT JOYSTICK
- Aim / Look

### RIGHT SHOULDER BUTTON
- Shoot

# Mobile / Touch
### LEFT JOYSTICK (visual)
- movement forward and back
- Strafe left and right

### RIGHT JOYSTICK (visual)
- Aim / Look
- Tap to shoot
